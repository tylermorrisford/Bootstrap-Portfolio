About Me

I am a front-end web developer, writer, and videographer, currently serving as the communications manager for ‘the J’. I’m also studying to be a full-stack developer, and while you’re probably thinking, “cool story, nerd!” I will put up a fight if you try to take my lunch money. I am fueled by granola bars and la croix, and I have a crippling weakness for fried foods, smoked meats, regular meats, bacon, smoked bacon, crispy bacon, the mere mention of bacon, fried foods served in concert with smoked meats and/or bacon, and bacon.

My sister and I were born in Gilroy, California, and having been there recently and noted the amazing growth, I wonder when they’ll start to refer to it as San Jose Heights. My dad was a commercial pilot, and despite the fact that he attended college on a football scholarship, he is basically a nerd in jock’s clothing. My mom was a professional ballet dancer, then teacher, then stayed home to raise some smelly kids, and dna tests have shown her to be primarily a nerd but also a hippie. My sister told on me a lot(twice), but she is now an amazing mother of 5 children that laughs just about always and lives on dr. pepper and dad jokes(like a nerd). I guess my point is that the (nerd)apple can’t fall far from the (nerd)tree if it’s surrounded on all sides by apple trees(and nerds). So to speak. I apologize if I just lost you.

A little bit about me, I have two very cute and very loud children, a beautiful wife who is also my best friend in the world, and sometimes feel like I can’t fathom why I would deserve any of these things unless in some past life I was a bird that flew into the path of a bullet that would’ve otherwise killed elton john. Pre-yellow brick road, obviously.
